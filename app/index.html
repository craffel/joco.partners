Travel

Depending on where you are staying, your best bet is to fly into San Francisco (SFO) or Oakland (OAK). You can take BART (Bay Area Rapid Transit, our “metro”) or Lyft/Uber from either. (We personally use Lyft and not Uber for a variety of reasons). If you are staying in the city, we recommend SFO. Please be aware that there is very bad rush hour traffic here (4-7), so be mindful of that if getting in soon before the Friday night dinner.

We strongly recommend *not* renting a car while you are here. Between ride apps and public transportation, there are plenty of ways to get around the city. Parking is usually very difficult, there are steep hills, and overly ambitious parking enforcement officers.
